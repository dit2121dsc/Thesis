In this paper, we demonstrate OmniCells: a cellularCSO-based indoor localization systemCSO designed to combat the device heterogeneity problem. OmniCells is a deep learningCSO-based system that leverages cellularCSO measurements from one or more training devices to provide consistent performance across unseen tracking phones. In this demo, we show the effect of device heterogeneity on the received cellularCSO signals and how this leads to performance deterioration of traditional localization systems. In particular, we show how OmniCells and its novel feature extraction methods enable learning a rich and device-invariant representation without making any assumptions about the source or target devices. The system also includes other modules to increase the deep model's generalization and resilience to unseen scenarios.
