In this work, we analyze the throughput of existing topologyCSO designs under different traffic patterns and study their unique advantages and potential costs in terms of bandwidthCSO and latency "tax". We show analytically and in simulations that Cerberus can improve throughput significantly compared to alternative approaches and operate datacenters at higher loads while being throughput-proportional.
